Technology Logics

Driven by our slogan, "Tech meets creativity," we have established a reputation for being more than just full-stack developers. Our innovative problem-solving strategy necessitates that we first establish a strong working relationship with our client(s). We take the business as our own and devote ourselves to producing bespoke user-friendly programs and websites. From its inception in 2021, Tech logics has successfully designed and delivered five VBA-based apps to schools and hotels around the country. We've also delivered a CSV-based Python application to a local phone shop that was having trouble keeping track of inventory. Our services also go beyond software development. We offer train local youngsters at no fee on how to develop websites and databases in order to equip them with marketable programming skills. With us, they study HTML, CSS, and Javascript for constructing responsive websites. We also train local farmers on basic computer packages to facilitate their day to day activities. Remember, you are not too young or too old to start programing! Where there is a will there is definetly a way.
